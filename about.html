About NayePankh Foundation

NayePankh Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering underprivileged communities through education, skill development, and social welfare initiatives.


Our Mission

To provide equal opportunities for learning, growth, and development to children and youth.


Our Vision

To build a society where every individual has access to education, skills, and opportunities for a better future.


Our Core Values

  • ✅ Integrity
  • ✅ Inclusion
  • ✅ Compassion
  • ✅ Innovation
